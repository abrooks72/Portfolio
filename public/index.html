About Me

Hi, I’m Austin Brooks — a technology professional with a background in software engineering who is now transitioning into instructional design. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science from San Diego State University, where I built a strong foundation in problem-solving, programming, and systems thinking. Over the years, I’ve discovered that my passion lies not only in building technology but also in helping others learn how to use it effectively. This led me to pursue additional certification programs at UC San Diego (Product Management) and UC Irvine (Instructional Design), where I strengthened my skills in designing learning experiences, managing projects, and creating engaging educational content. Today, I’m focused on blending my technical expertise with instructional design strategies to create impactful learning solutions that bridge the gap between technology and people. Whether it’s developing interactive courses, supporting learning platforms, or managing educational projects, I aim to make learning clear, engaging, and accessible.
